WHY US?

We care about personal space and comfort
Flying below full capacity in our baskets, we take a maximum of 16 guests.

Each day we choose the take-off site dependant on the wind direction to ensure the best and most enjoyable flight path for us all.

We fly once a day around sunrise, which offers the most favourable weather.

We guarantee minimums of one hour of flying time for the Basic Flight and one and a half hours for the Royal Flight

All our equipment is new
And custom-made for us at Cameron Balloons in the UK and Ultramagic in Spain;

new: Wifi on board.
